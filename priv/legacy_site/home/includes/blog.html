Awesometober Blogpost

Halloween, 2025
Booooo! October is generally a turbulent time for community servers due to Casual exsanguinating them but we've been thriving over here; who knows why? In the past two months or so we've added a variety of new features for players to check out, some of that includes:
  • !cwx has been expanded; You can use !cw to open the menu and !c or !cp to read what options are available for your class. (cp stands for custom weapons panel)
  • We've added a FastDL system to facilitate some of the new features; the first addition was a new building for the Engineer, the Amplifier, which provides a fire rate and reload speed buff to allies in a radius. Players can use !a to toggle the Amplifier, and in the case that sentries are disabled as they often are, sentries are replaced with Amplifiers instead of being destroyed!
  • The second FastDL feature is a system known as saysounds; players can use !soundname (for example !cow3) to play a sound for everyone to hear, with a list available at !sounds and a volume option with !vol 0-1
  • Our Steam Chat now has over 300 members which helps us organize play and to grow, join at any time to prove you're a true fan! (click this)
  • We've added actual custom weapons with some ideas the community have come up with; the first one is a pistol for Scout and Engineer which fires 35% faster, deals 40% less damage, is 40% more accurate, and has a 200% larger clip size; this emulates a "Lightning Gun" type weapon from classic FPS games. The second one is a cowboy revolver for Engineer and Scout's secondary slot that has an accuracy bonus and drains ammo from sentries, but the first shot deals a killing blow to anyone else holding the same weapon. The third new weapon is "Sproke", a drink for Scout, which makes the scout's magazine bottomless for 12 seconds, but leaves the Scout with no reserve ammo once the effect ends. Another new weapon is a fire shotgun for Pyro, which has a 30% damage penalty and a 15% smaller magazine size, but landing two shots that are calculated as accurate on a target within 5 seconds create a fiery explosion that ignites enemies and provides bonus damage. Two minor new weapons are the Wall Climbing Kit and Surfboard for Spy; the wall climbing kit removes your revolver, reduces HP by 10 and grants you the ability to climb walls with your melee attack, while the Surfboard is a sapper which reduces damage taken from sentries by 60% and has a 50% sapper damage penalty, allowing Spies to perform sentry surfing tricks more effectively.
  • The popular "Eientei" map has been given a high-budget Halloween reskin, including a Hell dimension from our famous MGE map.
  • We now have a Github repository for anyone to look at the server's programming under the hood, we've even had some contributions from friends to make new things possible, like nerfing the Wrangler shield's resistance level. Click the Github button on this page, or check it out here
    • Each new addition to the server is designed carefully and is subject to change based on data gathered from playtime, which allows us to slowly add new content and killer features while creating minimal aggrevation. Overall, the intent and purpose of the server is to be the best place to play, and new content like nerf reverts or new weapons are side attractions that happen to grow in number over time. Happy Halloween!

    Server Settings
    This server has specific settings per map, gamemodes and for certain playercount threshholds.
    Default Server Rules
    • Random crits & random melee crits are disabled
    • Random bullet spread is disabled
    • Default player limit is 28, but certain maps and gamemodes alter this
    • Default time limit is 50 minutes and default max rounds are 8
    • Default class limits allow 2 Heavy, 3 Medics, 3 Snipers, and 3 Spies per team unless overridden
    • Waiting for players time is usually disabled, setup times are reduced
    • Sprays and local mods are allowed, sv_pure is 0
    • This server has custom plugins for autobalance, scrambles and so on
    King of the Hill and Symmetrical Modes
    • KOTH maps have an extended timer in most cases
    • Respawn time is generally lower than asymmetrical modes
    • Sentries are usually disabled (replaced by Amplifiers) on KOTH
    Payload and Attack/Defend
    • Payload currently uses a 24 player visible cap, 60 minute timelimit, and 5 max rounds
    • Setup time is reduced by 15 seconds on average
    • Bhop is enabled during setup time (WIP)
    • Heavies are limited to 1 per team to prevent cart stacking
    Capture the Flag, Arena, and Special Modes
    • CTF has capture crits disabled, 9 flag captures per round, and short respawns
    • Arena disables first-blood crits and uses high max rounds with a shorter timelimit


    6. Currency
    This server has a currency system. You can acquire currency through gameplay actions like airshot kills and other badass stuff. you can use the currency to buy content packs such as Peter Griffin announcer pack, play in lotteries, send it to your friends, or spend it to create a clan/faction type of thing.
    Basic Commands
    • !shop, !store, or !buy opens the shop menu.
    • !bp, !bonus, !gems, or !gem shows a balance and the current gameplay reward summary.
    • !send, !sendbp, !bpsend, !sendgem, or !gemsend sends Gems to another player. The send command has a short cooldown.
    Earning Gems
    • +3 Gems: Medic Uber drops, penta-kills, and shutting down a killstreak of 20 or higher.
    • +2 Gems: Triple-kills, megakills, Soldier rocket sync kills, Pyro reflect direct hits, killstreak milestones above 10, and shutting down a killstreak of 15-19.
    • +1 Gem: Airshot kills, market garden kills, meatshot kills, Ambassador headshot kills, Pyro reflects, using UberCharge, killing the top-scoring player, 2+ dominations, revenge, Demoman sync kills, 5-10 killstreak milestones, and shutting down a killstreak of 7-14.
    • Per-map caps: Airshot kills 10, meatshot kills 10, market garden kills 5, top-scoring player kills 5, multiple dominations 5, killstreaks 5, multikills 5, killstreak shutdowns 5, Demo sync kills 5, UberCharges deployed 3, revenge 3, Soldier sync kills 3, welfare 1.
    • Multikill payouts: Triple-kill and megakill pay 2 Gems; penta-kill pays 3 Gems. A multikill payout uses one multikill cap slot, even when it pays more than one Gem.
    • Welfare: !welfare gives a random 4-16 Gems once per map when enabled.
    • Lottery: !lotto can pay out Gems from the active prize pool; higher ticket values give more chances to win.
    Welfare
    • !welfare, !collectwelfare, !handout, !gibs, and !welfarecheck are aliases.
    • Welfare is currently enabled above 11 players and can be collected once per map.
    • The current welfare payout is a random amount from 4 to 16 Gems. When a player collects welfare, monkey sounds begin to play.
    Lottery
    • !lotto, !lottery, !gamble, and !ticket open or buy into the active lottery.
    • Ticket values are rounded down to the nearest 5 Gems, and each 5 Gems is one chance to win.
    • The main winner receives most of the pool. For every 4 valid participants, an extra winner is added and receives 10% of the pool.
    • Players can view the prize pool or refund their current ticket before the draw starts.
    Current Shop Uses
    • Diamond Pickaxe: unlocks the Diamond Pickaxe custom weapon. This provides an all-class Diamond Pickaxe, which is a reskin of the Prinny Machete with the unique ability to random crit. Soldier has access to an Enchanted Diamond Pickaxe, which is an Equalizer that swings really fast. Both turn enemies to gold on kill.
    • Peter Griffin Announcer: unlocks 9 Peter Griffin sounds such as !children and acts as an announcer.
    • Autobalance Immunity (16 times): protects against selected autobalance moves until the uses run out.
    • Scramble Immunity (8 times): protects against selected scramble moves until the uses run out.


    7. Clans/Factions
    Clans is a plugin similar to Minecraft factions and other such systems. Create a clan by spending gems, invite your friends, create !tags to use in chat, declare war on other clans, use a private chat with /cc, and avoid autobalancing/scrambling when on a team with your clanmates.
    Basic Commands
    • !clan opens the clan menu, and !clans browses existing clans.
    • !clanhelp prints the in-game command summary.
    • !clancreate creates a clan. Clan creation costs 650 Gems when the store is available.
    • !clanleave leaves your clan; owners can delete their clan through the same flow.
    Membership
    • !claninvite invites a player, and !accept, !yes, or !deny handles pending invites.
    • !clanopen toggles whether players can join directly with !clanjoin.
    • !clankick removes a member if you have permission.
    • !clanmembers shows members, ranks, sub-tags, and clan war kills.
    Identity
    • !clantag sets the clan tag or your personal sub-tag.
    • !clandesc sets the clan description, and !clanrename renames the clan.
    • Clan tags are integrated with the tags system, so selected clan tags can appear beside names in-game.
    Stats and Wars
    • !claninfo shows clan details, and !clanpts or !clanpoints shows merged Whale Points for a clan.
    • !cc sends a private clan chat message.
    • !clanwar declares war on another clan or surrenders an active war. Clan wars target 50 kills and expire after a week.
    • !clanhistory shows recent clan history, such as joins and wars.


    8. Saysounds
    Saysounds let players play short server sounds by chat command, choose kill/death sounds, manage volume, and opt out of groups they do not want to hear.
    Basic Commands
    • !sounds or !saysounds lists configured groups first, then individual sounds.
    • !groups, !soundgroups, or !saysoundgroups lists only sound groups.
    • Typing a sound command directly, such as !pichuun, plays that sound. Typing a group command, such as !uma, plays a random sound from that group.
    • sm_saysound soundname and sm_saysound groupname work from console too.
    Personal Sound Choices
    • !killsounds and !diesounds open menus for kill and death sounds.
    • !killsound command and !diesound command set them directly.
    • Multiple comma-separated entries are valid, such as !killsound uma,pichuun; one eligible entry is picked randomly.
    • Group names and group aliases are valid in kill/death sound settings.
    Volume, Opt-Outs, and Paid Groups
    • !vol 0.0-1.0 changes your personal saysound volume.
    • !opt toggles saysounds globally, and !opts opens group toggles.
    • Paid saysound groups are marked in !sounds with [!shop]. They are only usable after buying the matching shop item.
    • Paid groups you own appear first in kill/death sound menus. Paid groups you do not own are hidden from those menus.
